          Differences from Tier 3

          Compared to Tier 3, Tier 4 adds a few more experimental roles. These include: Investigator, Witch, Priest, Prophet, Madman, and Spy. In addition I have buffed a couple of roles that I think are too weak or too unfun to include in setups frequently: Consigliere and Traitor. Both of these roles are marked with v2 to make it obvious that they are different from what players will expect.
